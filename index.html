Civil Engineering

Civil engineering involves the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure projects such as buildings, bridges, and roads. It covers areas like structural engineering, geotechnical engineering, and transportation engineering.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical engineering focuses on the design and manufacturing of mechanical systems and machines. It includes areas such as thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and robotics.

Electrical Engineering

Electrical engineering deals with the study of electrical systems and electronics. It covers subjects like circuit theory, power systems, and telecommunications.

Computer Engineering

A computer engineer is involved in both the design of computer architecture and the design of hardware and software. Working in this field involves learning programming languages, data structures, and artificial intelligence concepts.
